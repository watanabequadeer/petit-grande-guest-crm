# Petit Grande Marketing Strategy & Customer Management Meeting

## Marketing Strategy

- Continue Google Ads but position as broader approach, focusing on community building through social media (Instagram, Facebook, Line, WhatsApp, Discord)
- Leverage community platforms like Facebook Groups and Line Groups to reduce customer acquisition costs and facilitate repeat customers
- Implement content marketing strategy using Instagram Reels showing POV (point of view) experiences such as pickup service at Jumbay
- Consider TikTok as platform with easy algorithm penetration
- Facebook Marketplace effective for 40+ demographic

## Target Market & Service Proposals

- Primary targets: Thai and Filipino large families/groups (12-15 people)
- Comprehensive tour packages: accommodation + pickup/drop-off + Mt. Fuji tours + Disneyland, etc.
- Japan travel challenge: DIY approach is burdensome for large families and families with children
- Differentiation strategy: implement butler-style full support system like other countries (Philippines, Thailand)
- Regional collaboration possibilities: multi-destination tours leveraging Tokyo, Miyazaki, Niigata locations
- Strategy for seasonal destinations like Beppu: acquire repeat customers by leveraging Tokyo base

## CRM & Customer Database Development

- Exploring CRM development using ClickUp
- Existing customer database exists but not being utilized
- Importance of email collection: begin collecting email addresses from all guests at check-in
- Newsletter distribution, seasonal offers, repeat customer campaigns
- Need for email marketing metrics tracking (open rates, etc.)

## Customer Experience Improvement

- Conduct surveys and collect feedback from existing customers to identify improvement areas

- Focus on improving existing customer experience, not just acquiring new customers
